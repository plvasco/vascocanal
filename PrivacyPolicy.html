User Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Vasco Analytics allows users to request deletion of personal data associated with their account.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email us at support@vascoanalytics.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Please include:

  • Your full name.
  • The email address associated with your account.
  • A brief statement requesting deletion of your Vasco Analytics user data.

Processing Time

We will review and process valid deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.

What Will Be Deleted

We will delete or anonymize personal information associated with your Vasco Analytics account, unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes.

Confirmation

After your request is completed, we will send a confirmation email when possible.

Contact

Questions about data deletion may be sent to support@vascoanalytics.com.
